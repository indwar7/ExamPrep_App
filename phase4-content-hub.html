The Wonderful World of Science

Overview: Science is a way of thinking, observing and doing things to understand the world we live in and to uncover the secrets of the universe.

Learning Outcomes

  • Define science and understand its importance in daily life
  • Explain the scientific method with examples
  • Apply scientific thinking to everyday problems
  • Understand that anyone can be a scientist

Key Concepts

1. What is Science?
Science is a way of thinking, observing, and doing things to understand the world. It is characterized by curiosity about our surroundings.

2. The Scientific Method:
Five-step systematic approach: Observe → Wonder → Guess → Test → Analyze

📖 What is Science?

Science is a way of thinking, observing and doing things to understand the world we live in and to uncover the secrets of the universe. Think of it as a big adventure—we ask questions, explore the world and try to understand how things work.

🌟 The Journey Begins

As human beings, we have always been curious about our surroundings. We start exploring and asking questions right from childhood. Now, as you enter the Middle Stage, we continue this fascinating journey.

For this journey, the most important thing is to have Curiosity - which is where the title of this book comes from!

🔍 Science Everywhere

The most wonderful thing about science is that it is everywhere. From the depths of the ocean to the vastness of outer space, from what is cooking in the kitchen to what is happening on the playground, some of the most groundbreaking discoveries have often come from unexpected places.

Whether it is studying:

  • Tiny grains of sand or massive mountains
  • A leaf of grass or a vast forest
  • The behavior of stars or the growth of plants

There is always something new and exciting to discover!

📋 The 5 Steps of the Scientific Method

  1. OBSERVE - Look carefully at what interests you
  2. QUESTION - Think of questions about it
  3. HYPOTHESIS - Guess a possible answer
  4. TEST - Test through experiments
  5. ANALYSE - Understand the results

👨‍🔬 Who is a Scientist?

Anyone who follows the scientific method is working like a scientist!

  • A cook: Finding why food burns
  • A bicycle repair person: Finding why a tire is flat
  • An electrician: Finding why a light bulb isn't working
  • A farmer: Testing which fertilizer works best

When we try to ask questions and find out answers, we are all, in a way, scientists!

💪 To Be a Good Scientist...

The first and foremost thing is to be curious and observe your surroundings keenly.

The world is full of things we do not know, things that are waiting to be explored.

🤝 Important Note

Science is rarely done alone. Ask your friends to help you out! It is always more fun to discover things together.

🚀 Your Journey Begins Now!

Science is all about joyful exploration. Enjoy your scientific journey, keep exploring and never stop wondering!

After all, to be a wise person, you must be a "whys" person! ❓

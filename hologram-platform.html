Video Assessment Rubric

1. Content Accuracy (25%)

Does the video accurately explain the concept? Is information correct?

2. Clarity & Communication (25%)

Is the explanation clear and easy to understand? Good pace and delivery?

3. Visual Quality (20%)

Are visuals, demonstrations, or animations effective? Professional presentation?

4. Creativity & Engagement (20%)

Does the video engage viewers? Creative approaches to explaining concepts?

Student Video Submission Guidelines

Duration: 3-7 minutes

Format: MP4, MOV, or WebM (max 500MB)

Quality: Minimum 720p recommended

Content: Concept explanation, experiment demo, or creative visualization

Deadline: Submit by assessment date

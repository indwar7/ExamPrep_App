🔬 The 5 Magical Steps

🧑‍🔬
This is the SUPERPOWER!
Every scientist uses these 5 steps. Once you learn them, you can solve ANY mystery!

1️⃣ OBSERVE 👀

Look carefully at something. What do you see? What patterns do you notice? Write down what you find.

2️⃣ WONDER ❓

Ask questions! "Why?" "How?" "What if...?" Turn your observations into questions.

3️⃣ GUESS (Make a Hypothesis) 💭

Make an educated guess. Based on what you know, what do you think will happen? Write it as: "If... then..."

4️⃣ TEST 🧪

Do an experiment! Test your guess. Be careful. Measure. Record everything. Repeat it multiple times!

5️⃣ ANALYZE 📊

Look at your results. Was your guess correct? What did you learn? Even "wrong" answers teach us!

📖 Real Example: The Pen Problem!
Your pen stops writing. ✍️
Step 1: You observe it doesn't write.
Step 2: You ask "Why?"
Step 3: You guess "The ink is empty!"
Step 4: You open the pen and check.
Step 5: You discover the ink WAS empty! 🎉

🌱 Experiment 1: Plant Growth & Light

Question: Do plants grow taller with more sunlight?

You Need:

  • 3 pots with soil
  • Plant seeds (beans work!)
  • Water
  • Ruler for measuring
  • Different light spots (sunny, shade, dark)

Steps:

1. Plant same seeds in 3 pots with same soil & water
2. Put Pot 1 in sunny window ☀️
3. Put Pot 2 in shady corner 🌤️
4. Put Pot 3 in dark closet 🌑
5. Water equally every 2-3 days
6. Measure height every week for 4 weeks
7. Compare your results!

🎯 What Will Happen: Sunny plants grow tallest! Dark plants don't grow much. Why? Plants need light for energy!

💧 Experiment 2: Water State Changes!

Question: What happens when water gets hot?

Steps:

1. Put ice in a cup 🧊 (SOLID - hard & cold)
2. Heat it slowly - watch ice melt 💧 (LIQUID - flows!)
3. Keep heating - see steam rise ☁️ (GAS - invisible!)

🎯 Amazing Discovery: Water changes form! Cold = solid, warm = liquid, hot = gas!
